CLOUDFLARE PAGES - PASSOS
=========================

1) Conectar o repo Git no Cloudflare Pages.
2) Build command: deixar em branco.
3) Build output directory: "/".
4) Root directory: "/".
5) Em Settings > Environment variables, criar:
   - EMAIL_API_URL
   - EMAIL_API_KEY
   - EMAIL_FROM
   - EMAIL_TO
6) Deploy. O endpoint /api/contact vira da functions/api/contact.js.
